####################################################################################################
Reincarnation
####################################################################################################

== Background ==

In this project, the background story is that a student stayed up to study for the test, when an
accident happened that he or she got a second chance to choose his or her life by getting a god
quiz. In this quiz, the student will find that his or her soul gets trapped in an isolated and
abandoned field . To leave here, the student needs to reach a cliff by escaping room and a huge
maze. Then use cloud to reach the cross
the cliff to end this quiz

== Level Information ==

For each level in this project, the full mark is 100. At the end of the game, you will get a total
 score from 0 to 300. Here are the brief information the quiz:

* Escape Room (level 1): A simple escape room game. Within limit number of tries,  find keys, number
cards and any hint in this space to figure out the password of the door and get out of here.

* Maze (level 2): A simple maze game. More instructions can refer to hint page located in right-upper
corner of pause page in game. Notice that a bug can occur if clicked on screen while loading, this
can be fixed simply quit and enter again.

* Frogger (level 3): A simple frogger game. You can find hints of this game inside pause page.

== Game Result ==

The result of your reincarnation quiz depending on your score list as follows:
    * 0-100: Fish in the fish tank;
    * 100-200: Baby;
    * 200-300: Yourself;

####################################################################################################
Note:
####################################################################################################

* For each level,  there will always be one score stored in the corresponding level and the score is
  the latest score the player got.
* If player exists in the middle of the game in each level, the player will not receive the score.
